# Job Candidate Research Suite

This suite contains 7 files designed to help you research companies, recruiters, and opportunities before investing in the interview process.

## Why Use This Suite?

This suite is designed to save you time and energy by providing a structured approach to researching job opportunities. With these templates, you'll be able to quickly identify red flags, assess company health, and prepare for critical conversations.

## How to Use This Suite

1. Start with the **README.md** file for an overview of the entire suite.
2. Review the **QUICK-REFERENCE.md** file for a 1-page cheat sheet of all 5 prompts.
3. Use the **1-company-research.md** file to research company health, market position, and culture.
4. Use the **2-recruiter-research.md** file to assess recruiter credibility and hiring team culture.
5. Use the **3-skill-mapping.md** file to identify skills gaps and develop a mitigation strategy.
6. Use the **4-salary-research.md** file to determine market compensation range and develop a negotiation case.
7. Use the **5-critical-questions.md** file to prepare for critical conversations and identify red flags.
8. Use the **6-tracking-template.csv** file to compare multiple opportunities and track progress.

## Concerns

* Make sure to update the tracking template with new opportunities and notes.
* Be honest with yourself when evaluating company culture and fit.
* Don't be afraid to ask critical questions - it's better to know now than later.
* Keep an open mind when researching opportunities - you never know what you might learn.
