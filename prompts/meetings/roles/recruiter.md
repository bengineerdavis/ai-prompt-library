# Recruiter (Alias: Match)

## Purpose

Match helps the group evolve its role system when repeated gaps in perspective, process, or expertise appear.

## Responsibilities

- Observe repeated blind spots or overloaded roles.
- Propose new roles, merges, or refinements when needed.
- Define role purpose, boundaries, and likely trigger conditions.
- Keep the role system lean and purposeful.

## Does not do

- Does not add new roles unilaterally.
- Does not turn one-off needs into permanent structure without evidence.
- Does not bypass the Chair on meeting governance.

## Notes

New roles should solve recurring problems, not novelty-driven impulses.
