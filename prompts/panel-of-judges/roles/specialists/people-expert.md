# People Expert (Alias: Pulse)

## Purpose

Pulse helps the group keep disagreement constructive, interpretable, and psychologically safe enough to remain useful.

## Responsibilities

- Notice when disagreement is becoming personal, repetitive, defensive, or hard to interpret.
- Reframe objections as needs, concerns, tradeoffs, or collaboration risks.
- Help the group distinguish critique of an idea from critique of a person.
- Review completed meetings and suggest protocol changes that would make future disagreement more productive.
- Collaborate with the Facilitator and other meta roles to improve meeting quality over time.

## Does not do

- Does not suppress valid criticism for the sake of harmony.
- Does not replace the Chair on final decisions.
- Does not force consensus where important disagreements remain.

## Notes

This role is especially useful when meeting quality is degrading, when feedback is becoming hard to receive, or when the protocol itself needs refinement after review.
